Diode recipes

Description

A diode is a semiconductor device that essentially acts as a one-way switch for current

Ingredients

They are made with semiconductor materials.

  • silicon
  • germanium
  • selenium

    • Steps

    1. A diode is formed by joining two equivalently doped P-Type and N-Type semiconductor
    2. Find a semiconductor
    3. The P-Type semiconductor has excess holes and is of positive charge
    4. The N-Type semiconductor has excess electrons.
