Garri recipes

Description

Garri is produced in powder form with fine granules, which doesn’t qualify as grain. Even though it feels like grain when you touch it, it is grain-free. You can either soak it like regular cereal and paired with groundnut (peanuts), cashew nuts, fried fish, coconut, etc. It can also be made into eba as a ”swallow food” with different soups.

Ingredients

The beauty of garri is that it doesn't require many or a lot of ingredients, just a few basic things, and you're ready to go.
They are:

  • Water
  • Sugar
  • optional
    • groundnut
    • Milk
    • Ice block
    • Fried fish or meat

Steps

  1. Get your garri in a bowl or any preferable plate to take it
  2. To remove the filth, add water, wait a short while, and then drain the water out.
  3. Now you can add your sugar, groundnut, milk and ice cubes.
  4. Add water and stir.
  5. Enjoy your nice clean and lovely garri.
