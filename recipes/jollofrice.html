Description

Jollof or jollof rice, is a rice dish from West Africa. where the best is made only in Nigeria

Ingredients

The ingredients used to make jollof rice vary based on who is making it and where they are from. The ingredients used to prepare jollof rice in Nigeria are listed below.

  • Rice
  • Tomato paste
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Red bell pepper
  • Scotch bonnet peppers
  • Spices
  • salt
